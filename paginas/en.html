Santiago Cisternas

Web development & design

Hello! My name is Santiago. I'm an antrhopologist. My strong background in social science research (I was a researcher at the Applied Ethnography Laboratory of UNLP for 2 years) and my interest in exploring new possibilities led me to the world of UX. I gained expertise in this field through courses, which allowed me to carry out design projects (ARTIX) and research projects (Uber).

"And how did you get into the development world?" you might be wondering. Well, through scientific outreach on the Internet. In 2022 and 2023, I worked at Argonautas del Atlántico Sur, an outrech online project. Wefaced with the project's need for its own platform, I decided to take the lead and learned about front-end development. You can see the result here. Since I started learning front-end development until today, I have learned various technologies: HTML5, CSS3, Sass, Bootstrap 5, JavaScript, React, Node.js and MongoDB.

All this journey allows me to have, nowadays, a broad vision of the product journey, from initial planning to the final launch.

Languages, frameworks y libraries

  • HTML5 - Project planning and broad knowledge of various tags: semantic, lists, forms, buttons, selection controls, tables, multimedia tags.
  • CSS3 - Box modeling: flexbox & GRIDS, mediaqueries, pseudo-classes, basic animations, SEO. Proficient in SASS.
  • JavaScript - Functions and DOM manipulation. Fetch API. Object-Oriented Programming (OOP). React: NPM. Virtual DOM and JSX. Hooks, Axios, React Router, and Hooks context.

Tools

  • Git - Creation and management of local repositories and branches.
  • GitHub - Creation and management of remote repositories
  • Figma - My main tool for interface design and prototyping.
  • Adobe Illustrator - If necessary, I create custom images for my projects using this tool, such as logos or illustrations.

My work

Argonautas

Are you interested in social and natural sciences? Argonautas del Atlántico Sur is a project dedicated to the outreach of these disciplines through its website, featuring blog posts, and Es Más Complejo podcast. The website is fully responsive.

Uber

One of the main objectives of companies like Uber is to safeguard the safety of both providers and consumers. However, over the years, we have witnessed numerous acts of violence that raise concerns among users, especially if they are women. For this reason, I set out to investigate how safe Uber users feel and what factors affect that perception, in order to propose solutions tailored to their needs.

Artix

If you ever find yourself unaware of the events happening in your city, you've come to the right place. Artix is an app that allows you to discover, through geolocation, all the events near you, so you can make the most of your weekend.
